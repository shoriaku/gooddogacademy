Who Am I?

I recived a Bachelors of Science degree majoring in Behavioral Sciences at Appalachain State where I furthered my knowledge and passion for animals. My passion for dog training wasn’t fully realized until I rescued my very own dog, Tater. He was malnourished, dehydrated, and in desperate need of medical attention. I quickly decided to rescue him. I am an enthusiastic instructor and avid agility competitor. I am so excited about the progress made and being made by my DTW students and look forward to helping them achieve their goals regardless of whether it means spending time with their dog on the agility course or competing at agility trials.

What does General Training Inlcude?

A dog trainer can provide basic obedience training with the owner, or provide training for specialized activities such as search and rescue, hunting, law enforcement, working with livestock, therapy, entertainment, dog sports, protection and detection.

What Do I Do As A General Trainer?

Most of dog training entails teaching people; the vast majority of my work is done by teaching classes and private lessons for pet-dog guardians. The success of a dog’s training program depends upon the owners compliance with that program.Individualized training is used with dogs that have a unique training problem such as aggression, hyperactivity, fear, separation anxiety, inappropriate elimination, excessive barking, insecurity, destructive behaviours, and biting. I am very excited to get to know you and your four legged friend!
